PATENTS and SCIENTIFIC STUDIES

 

2013                           Touch Operated Mechanical Locking Mechanism

                                    Patent No: 2013/14317

 

The invention relates to a touch operated mechanical locking mechanism. The invention is a locking mechanism which can be operated with touch especially for those who have disabilities or 5 who have not enough strength to handle and manually rotate certain parts such as keys and handles. The touch operated mechanical locking mechanism does not require manual handling of the user as it does not require any accessories including handles and keys.

The components on locking mechanisms with handles and keys are handled by a high number  of users, even people pay attention to washing their hands at lavatories, they are contaminated with the 10 microbes on the locking systems previously used by other users. These locking mechanisms are a great concern in terms of hygiene at public lavatories at hospitals, patient rooms and shopping malls etc. where they are used by many. As it does not require any key, it does not have a key hole. Therefore, it does not allow disposition of microbes as with these key holes. As there is only a single button left outside the system, the area which needs cleaning is minimized.
